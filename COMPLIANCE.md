# Compliance Strategy

## Current Position

TransportVault is pursuing a FIPS 140-oriented architecture.

Current goals:

- Strong encryption
- Audit logging
- Verification workflows
- Chain-of-custody support
- Secure deployment operations

## Future Goals

- NIST 800-171 alignment
- RMF support documentation
- STIG hardening guidance
- Compliance evidence collection

## Important

TransportVault should not be represented as formally compliant,
certified, validated, or authorized unless documented evidence exists.
